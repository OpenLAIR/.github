We are interested in making machine learning and deep learning algorithms more **reliable, robust, efficient, and interpretable**. Our research spans:

- **Trustworthy AI** — robustness, fairness, privacy, and safety of AI systems
- **Medical AI** — AI-powered healthcare and clinical applications
- **Generative AI / LLM** — large language models, AIGC, and their responsible deployment
- **Federated Learning** — privacy-preserving collaborative machine learning
- **Reinforcement Learning** — efficient and safe decision-making algorithms
